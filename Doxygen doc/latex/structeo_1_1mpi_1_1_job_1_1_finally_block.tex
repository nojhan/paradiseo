\subsubsection*{template$<$class Job\+Data$>$\newline
struct eo\+::mpi\+::\+Job$<$ Job\+Data $>$\+::\+Finally\+Block}

Finally block of the main algorithm. 

Herb Sutter\textquotesingle{}s trick for having a finally block, in a try/catch section\+: invoke a class at the beginning of the try, its destructor will be called in every cases.

This implements the end of the master algorithm\+:
\begin{DoxyItemize}
\item sends to all available workers that they are free,
\item waits for last responses, handles them and sends termination messages to last workers. 
\end{DoxyItemize}
